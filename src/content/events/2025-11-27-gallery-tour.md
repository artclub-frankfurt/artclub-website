We are delighted to announce our very first event, a gallery tour on Thursday, November 27!

Join us as we visit different galleries around Fahrgasse, where the gallerists will personally guide us through their current exhibitions. Afterwards, we will end the evening together at a nearby bar.

The meeting point is Fischerplätzchen, 60311 Frankfurt, at 4 PM. The tour will last until 6 PM.
