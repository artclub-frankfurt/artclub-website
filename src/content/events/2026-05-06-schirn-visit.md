We are visiting the Schirn Kunsthalle for a guided tour of Thomas Bayrle's current solo exhibition. After the tour we will continue at Café KoZ.

## Registration

Free for members, €5 for non-members. Registration opens 1 May and is binding. If you cannot attend, please cancel in time so someone else can take your spot. A waitlist is available if the event is full.

## Good to know

- Meeting point: in front of the Schirn's main entrance.
- Open to students only — please bring your student ID.
