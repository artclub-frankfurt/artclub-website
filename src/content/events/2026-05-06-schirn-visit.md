We are visiting the SCHIRN KUNSTHALLE together on Wednesday, May 6, for a guided tour of Thomas Bayrle's current solo exhibition. We will meet at 5.30 PM in front of the SCHIRN entrance, and the tour will last until 7 PM. After the tour we will continue at Café KoZ.

## Registration

Participation is free for our members, but registration is required as spots are limited. Registration opens May 1 and is binding. A waitlist is available if the event is full. If you cannot attend, please cancel in time so someone else can take your spot. 

Please note that this event is open to students only, so don’t forget to bring your student ID.
(Open events for non-students will follow again soon.)
