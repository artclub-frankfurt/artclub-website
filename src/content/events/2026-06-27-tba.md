Following the great response to our first Gallery Tour, we're excited to announce our second edition!

Join us on Saturday, June 27, as we explore some of Frankfurt's leading contemporary art galleries. At each stop, the gallerists will introduce us to their current exhibitions and share insights into the artists and works on view.

Our stops:

1. Galerie FILIALE
2. Galerie Monica Ruppert
3. Gregor Aigner / irritate modern
4. Galerie Bärbel Grässlin

The tour takes place from 12 to 2 PM. Afterwards, everyone is welcome to join us for drinks and a chance to continue the conversation.

We're looking forward to an exciting afternoon filled with art and good conversation!



### Registration

Participation is free, but registration is required. As spaces are limited, we kindly ask that you only register if you're able to attend. If your plans change, please let us know as early as possible so we can offer your spot to someone else.

### Meeting point

In front of Galerie FILIALE at 12:00 PM

Stiftstraße 9, 60313 Frankfurt am Main
